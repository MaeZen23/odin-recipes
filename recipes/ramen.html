Ramen

A bowl of tasty ramen!

Take this easy recipe up a notch by adding a bit of zing to your favourite quick-cook packaged ramen with a homemade chicken (or veg) broth, using garlic and ginger, and topped with fresh herbs and veggies!

Awesome ramen veggies include: carrots, shiitake mushrooms, kale, or anything that takes your fancy!

And don't forget the yummy toppings like panko, egg, chives, and chilli oil!

Here's how to make it:

Ingredients

  • 1 Tblsp Sesame oil
  • 3 tsp Grated ginger
  • 4 tsp Grated garlic
  • 4C Broth (chicken or veg)
  • 4C Water
  • 1 ounce Dried shiitake mushrooms
  • 2packs Instant ramen noodles
  • 1/2C Chopped scallions/chives
  • 2C chopped Kale
  • 1C Shredded Carrots
  • Sriracha to taste
  • Golden panko or any other desired topping

Steps

  1. Heat the sesame oil in large skillet, over medium low heat. Add the garlic and ginger. Stir fry for 2 mins until soft and fragrant.
  2. Add the broth and water. Bring to a simmer. Add the mushrooms and simmer for about 10 minutes, until soft and broth is flavourful.
  3. Add the instant noodles to the hot broth and simmer for 5 minutes or until the noodles have softened. Add the scallions and stir to combine.
  4. Remove from heat, stir in kale and carrots, and top with your chosen toppings. Season with chilli oil, hot sauce, sesame oil, or soy sauce.
