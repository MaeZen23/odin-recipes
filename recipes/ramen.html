Ramen

A bowl of tasty ramen!

Take the usual ramen up a notch with this quick homemade ramen. Fresh veggies and herbs make this extra delicious, healthy, and cozy!

This easy recipe uses your favourite quick cook packaged ramen and adds a bit of zing to it with your homemade chicken or veg broth using garlic and ginger.

A great idea would be to use a selection of the fresh veggies you have on hand, for garnishing, but also to add flavour to the broth! Great ramen veggies include: carrots, shiitake mushrooms, kale, or anything that takes your fancy!

Don't forget the yummy toppings like panko, egg, or chilli oil!

Here's how to make it:

Ingredients

  • 1 Tblsp Sesame oil
  • 3 tsp Grated ginger
  • 4 tsp Grated garlic
  • 4C Broth (chicken or veg)
  • 4C Water
  • 1 ounce Dried shiitake mushrooms
  • 2 packs Instant Ramen noodles
  • 1/2C Chopped scallions/chives
  • 2C chopped Kale
  • 1C Shredded Carrots
  • Sriracha to taste
  • Golden panko or any other desired topping

Steps

  1. Heat the sesame oil in large skillet, over medium low heat. Add the garlic and ginger. Stir fry for 2 mins until soft and fragrant.
  2. Add the broth and water. Bring to a simmer. Add the mushrooms and simmer for about 10 minutes, until soft and broth is flavourful.
  3. Add the instant noodles to hot the broth and simmer for 5 minutes or until the noodles have softened. Add the scallions and stir to combine.
  4. Remove from heat, stir in kale and carrots, top with your chosen toppings. Season with chilli oil, hot sauce, sesame oil, or soy sauce.
